
Responsible Social Media Checklist
👍 Do’s
✔️ Think before you post — ensure it’s respectful and appropriate
✔️ Protect your privacy (use strong passwords & privacy settings)
✔️ Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news)
✔️ Be respectful in comments and discussions
✔️ Use social media for learning, networking, and growth
❌ Don’ts
❌ Don’t share personal/sensitive information publicly
❌ Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online harassment
❌ Don’t believe or spread unverified news or rumors
❌ Don’t waste excessive time scrolling (avoid addiction)
❌ Don’t post anything that could harm your reputation or future career
