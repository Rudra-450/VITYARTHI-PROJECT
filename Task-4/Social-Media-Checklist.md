
## Responsible Social Media Checklist
# For College Students

## 👍 Do’s
  1. Think before you post — ensure it’s respectful and appropriate
  2. Protect your privacy (use strong passwords & privacy settings)
  3. Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news)
  4. Be respectful in comments and discussions
  5. Use social media for learning, networking, and growth
## ❌ Don’ts
1. Don’t share personal/sensitive information publicly
2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online harassment
3. Don’t believe or spread unverified news or rumors
4. Don’t waste excessive time scrolling (avoid addiction)
5. Don’t post anything that could harm your reputation or future career
