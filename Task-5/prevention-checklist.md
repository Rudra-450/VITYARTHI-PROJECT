# ✅ Stay Safe Online 
## Checklist for College Students (India)

## 🔐 General Online Safety
1. Use strong, unique passwords for every account (combine letters, numbers, symbols).
2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on apps like Google Pay, Paytm, and Instagram.
3. Never click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.
4. Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into banking or important accounts.
6. Regularly update your phone and apps to fix security vulnerabilities.
7. Don’t overshare personal details (address, phone, college ID) on social media.
8. Always log out from shared/public computers (labs, libraries, cyber cafés).

---
## 💸 UPI & Financial Safety (Important ⚠️)
1. Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.
2. Don’t approve “Collect Request” payments unless you trust the sender—receiving money does NOT require entering your PIN.
3. Double-check the UPI ID or QR code before sending money.
4. Enable transaction alerts on your banking apps for instant updates.

-----
## 🚨 If Something Goes Wrong
1. Immediately report fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
2. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available across India)
