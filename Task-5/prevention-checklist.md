# ✅ Stay Safe Online 
## Checklist for College Students (India)

## 🔐 General Online Safety
☐ Use strong, unique passwords for every account (combine letters, numbers, symbols).
☐ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on apps like Google Pay, Paytm, and Instagram.
☐ Never click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.
☐ Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
☐ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into banking or important accounts.
☐ Regularly update your phone and apps to fix security vulnerabilities.
☐ Don’t overshare personal details (address, phone, college ID) on social media.
☐ Always log out from shared/public computers (labs, libraries, cyber cafés).

---
## 💸 UPI & Financial Safety (Important ⚠️)
☐ Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.
☐ Don’t approve “Collect Request” payments unless you trust the sender—receiving money does NOT require entering your PIN.
☐ Double-check the UPI ID or QR code before sending money.
☐ Enable transaction alerts on your banking apps for instant updates.

-----
## 🚨 If Something Goes Wrong
☐ Immediately report fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
☐ Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available across India)
